Neuro care unit
  • ABOUT US

This is a five bedded unit for the support of early strokes, seizure disorders, neuro and spine trauma and other neurologic conditions. It is equipped with state - of - art patient monitors, ventilators, non invasive respiratory devices and intracranial pressure monitoring system etc. An expert team of neurosurgeons, neurologists, cardiologists and general physicians are AVAILABLE round the clock and they are ably assisted by trained nursing and other paramedical staff. The Unit gives combined neurological, cardiological, physiotherapy, speech and rehabilitative support to patients . The Neuro care Unit is located close to MRI and CT scan units , thus facilitating easy access to radiological and other investigations .
